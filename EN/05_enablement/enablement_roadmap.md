## Phase 2: Certification (Week 3-4)

### SnowPro Core Certification

The SnowPro Core certification is the recommended starting point for **all profiles**.

**Details:**
- **Format:** Online exam (65 questions, 115 minutes)
- **Cost:** $175 USD
- **Preparation:** 2-3 weeks of study
- **Validity:** 2 years

**Exam topics:**
1. Snowflake Cloud Data Platform Features & Architecture
2. Account Access and Security
3. Performance Concepts
4. Data Loading and Unloading
5. Data Transformations
6. Data Protection and Data Sharing

**Preparation resources:**
- Official study guide: https://learn.snowflake.com/courses/uni-spcc-preparation
- Documentation: https://docs.snowflake.com
- Practice on the trial account

### Advanced Certifications (after Core)

| Certification | Target Profile | Prerequisite |
|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| SnowPro Advanced: Data Architect | Architects | SnowPro Core |
| SnowPro Advanced: Data Engineer | Data Engineers | SnowPro Core |
| SnowPro Advanced: Data Analyst | Analysts | SnowPro Core |
| SnowPro Advanced: Administrator | Admins/DBAs | SnowPro Core |

**Recommendation:** Nominate 2-3 team members to complete SnowPro Core within the next 4 weeks.

## Phase 3: Implementation (Month 2)

### Develop an Internal Demo Asset

Create a demonstration that you can use with your clients:

1. **Choose a scenario** relevant to your market
   - Example: Retail analytics, Financial reporting, IoT data processing
2. **Implement in Snowflake**
   - Database + sample data
   - 2-3 transformations (Dynamic Tables or dbt)
   - 1 governance policy
   - 1 Cortex AI function
3. **Document and test**

### Identify First POC

- Select 1-2 clients with potential for Snowflake
- Define a specific and measurable use case
- Align with the Snowflake team for technical support

### Define Internal Plays

Create internal "plays" within your organization:
- **Data Warehouse Modernization Play** - Migration from legacy to Snowflake
- **Governance Play** - Implement Horizon for clients with compliance requirements
- **AI Play** - Demonstrate Cortex AI for data analysis and NLP
- **Applications Play** - Build Streamlit applications on top of data in Snowflake
