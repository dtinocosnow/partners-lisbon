# Snowflake AI Data Cloud - Partner Enablement Session

---

**Location:** Lisbon, Portugal
**Duration:** 3 hours

---

## Session Objective

Provide our partners with a complete and practical overview of the Snowflake AI Data Cloud platform, enabling them to:

- Understand the architecture and fundamental pillars of the platform
- Observe key functionalities in action through live demonstrations
- Explore AI capabilities and native applications
- Complete guided hands-on exercises to consolidate knowledge
- Define a clear enablement path and next steps

**Target Audience:** Data Analysts, Data Engineers, Architects and other technical profiles
